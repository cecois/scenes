COLD OPEN

INT. MONICA'S APARTMENT - DAY

Joey is assembling a very modest house of cards, television playing behind him. Enter Monica and Chandler, removing light jackets.

CHANDLER
Hey, Joe.

Check it out!
MONICA
What's this?
JOEY

JOEY
Ooooooh - I get that expression now! No, actually I’m just up for a role as an architect.

Joey stares ahead as though his statement needs no explanation.

CHANDLER (TO MONICA)

Joey, have you seen Phoebes? I need to talk to her.
JOEY
Yeah, she's right over ‑‑ oh, you know what? Yesterday she was right over there.
CHANDLER
Told ya you should have asked yesterday.
JOEY
Ok, sure. But if I see her it'll be here - I'm going to build an addition.
CHANDLER
Ah. Real estate never sleeps. Wait, I thought you had an aud-ition today.
JOEY
That was canceled.
CHANDLER
Your audition was canceled? Why?
JOEY
Yeah, Estelle called and said they gave the part to Anthony Hopkins and don't bother coming in.
MONICA
You were up for the same part as Anthony Hopkins?!
JOEY
Yeah, but get this:
JOEY (CONT'D)
He's supposedly more ~scholarly~ than I am!

Joey waves arms in frustration. The gesture knocks over the card house.

JOEY (CONT'D)
Aw dammit!

Joey slams his fist on remains of card house, destroying what remained.

CHANDLER (TO MONICA)
How different would that movie have been?

ACT I

INTERIOR - CENTRAL PERK - DAY

Rachel enters. She looks around, sees only Gunther.

RACHEL
Hey Gunther. Have you seen Phoebe?
GUNTHER
Tall blonde one, right? With the guitar?
RACHEL
Gunther! What made you want to be funny today?
GUNTHER
Rachel, I haven't seen her in probably three years.
RACHEL
What are you talking about? We're in here all the time together.
GUNTHER
I sold this place years ago, Rachel. Did you not notice I'm never here?
RACHEL
Oh (clears throat) well of course I noticed that. Wait, you owned this place?!
GUNTHER
I haven't seen you in three years, either.
RACHEL
(jokingly punching Gunther's arm)
How ya been, pal?
GUNTHER
Well, it hasn't been the easiest transit‑‑

Ross enters.

ROSS (MOPILY)
Hi.
RACHEL
Ooh, Ross. Have you seen Phoebe? I need to talk to her.
ROSS
No I haven't, sorry. About what?
RACHEL
I guess I can tell you.

Rachel pulls Ross away from Gunther, who is noticeably miffed and resumes what he was doing.

RACHEL (CONT'D)
I think Mike might be cheating on her.
ROSS
What?! Mike? No way.
RACHEL
Yeah, well i saw him with a brunette woman and he had his (clears throat, moves in for a secret) hand on her ass.
ROSS
What? No! Wait, a 'brunette woman'?
RACHEL
Well I only saw her from behind. But that's where her ass is and his hand was on it - just walking down the street that way.
ROSS (SHAKEN)
This is unbelievable. That Mike would do that. Also: that anyone would walk down the street that way. There must be some explanation.
RACHEL
I hope so, too. But that's why I need to find Phoebe.
ROSS
Well did you ask Gunther? If she's been around at all today he would have seen her.

Rachel tries to interrupt Ross but fails.

ROSS (CONT'D)
Gunther, has Phoebe been around at all today?
GUNTHER
I don't know - I don't work here.
ROSS
Right, right, I know - you own it. But you would have seen her if she was here, right?

Gunther exits wordlessly. Ross shrugs to Rachel.

EXT. CENTRAL PERK - DAY

Monica and Chandler are blocked from entry by Gunther storming out.

CHANDLER
Taking the day off, eh?

MONICA
Jeesh.
CHANDLER
Do you think he's angry that we don't spend as much time here anymore?

Monica shrugs, they enter the shop to find Ross and Rachel.

MONICA
Rachel! Ross! Oh my god! We haven't been here forever and look who we find.

They all greet each other.

RACHEL
This is so great, you guys. Ross and I were just in the neighborhood looking for Phoebe.

That's so funny - I'm looking for her, too. I hope she's alright.

Enter Joey from the back of the restrooms.

CHANDLER
Oh - maybe Joe knows.

Oh my god - Joey!

raucous greetings

Oh. My. God.

You guys! I didn't think you still hung out here. None of you have met my littlest ones. This is little Jacob Alphonse Rabinowitz III. And this one is Bill.

"Bill"?

braying laughter

Yeah, you made that joke last time.

It's so good to see you, Janice. What brings you to the city?
JANICE
Well we wanted to visit daddy in his new office but I don't go below 15th.

JANICE (CONT'D)

Strictly a Harlem girl, then?

braying laughter

Well...

So Janice, it's great to see you but we're kind of all looking for Phoebe. Have you maybe seen her?
JANICE
I haven't, sweetie, I'm sorry. But if I see her I'll tell her.
RACHEL
Thanks, Janice.

JANICE (TO THE GROUP)
Excuse me. Bill, honey - that's Gunther. We don't kick him.

RACHEL

Monica, it's dad. Hello? Hello?

MONICA
He's calling me, too! Hello? Dad? Hello? What?! Dad, I can't understand you. Dad? Dad?!

I don't know!

Phoebe?
